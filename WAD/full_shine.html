Full Shine

Detailed description of the Full Shine service.

This service includes deep exterior cleaning, polish and protective finish to restore gloss and remove minor imperfections. Decontamination Wash: Proffessionals ude a "two-bucket method" with grit guards to prevent scratching, followed by a clay bar treatment to pull out embedded contaminants (like tar and tree sap) that a standard wash misses. Paint Correction & Polishing: Buffing with an electric polisher removes swirl marks, and oxidation to reveal a deep reflective shine. Surface Protection: A durable layer of wax, paint sealant
